In order to upload to CCP4 cloud, we need your CCP4 Cloud username, and your CloudRun Id.
You can find your CloudRun Id on the 'My Account' page of CCP4 Cloud.

Once uploaded, select the task 'Import from Cloud Storage' in your CCP4 Cloud project.
Click 'Select file(s)', then click 'My Cloud Storage'.
